My name is John and living the simple life saves my life

I used to be pack rat. I had so many things that I thought that I loved, but the truth was I couldn't even appreciate any of it because there was too much.

Things wew terrible. I had a job I hated, a home that I didn't want to live in, and I couldn't hold a relationship.

How I turned things around.

I was on the verge of a breakdown when I got rid of everything. I litterally out 90% of my possession in the garbage , sold my place and moved into a smaller apartment, and I quit my job.

It wasn't wasy at all, but everything around me was in a bad place. I didn't know what else to do. And it worked

Now I live simple life

How that I'm living a simple life, things are so much better.I'm less stressed, enjoy life and work more, and I have more free time to enjoy.

With how much ut turned my life around. I felt like I had no choice but to start sharing how I did it. and how others can benefit fromo living a simple life
